Sears Commercial One Dun & Bradstreet and Equifax Small Business Sears Commercial One Card can save your company time and money when purchasing Kenmore appliances. lawn tractors. If you have strong history then they may not require a personal guarantor. If your company is less than 2 years old then they may require a guarantor unless your business has exceptional credit. Be ready to supply trade references if needed. When submitting your application do not supply a personal guarantor. After you submit your application they will notify you in writing of your results within 7-10 business days.

Approval Requirements: Must provide a personal guarantor unless you are a company that has over $5 million in revenues and in business for at least 3 years. Government entities and Non-profits do not have to supply a personal guarantor. You will need to supply 4 trade references and have at least an 80 Paydex Score.

They will check 411 listing. secretary of state for status of your corporation or LLC to make sure it's in good standing. You'll need to supply your EIN. copy of a voided business check. copy of a utility bill showing the business address and phone number. and a copy of your business license. (if a business license is required in your state) Leave the personal guarantor section blank.

With established business credit history and good trade references you will not need to supply a personal guarantor. If your company has little to no credit history then you will need to supply one.
